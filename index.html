Stonefly Lodge Newsletter – May 2019

The 2018 /2019 season started with a bang last year producing a trophy trout (10.5IB) in the first week. We experienced a very dry summer which kept the river levels low and the water very clear most of the season. Fortunately no major floods and only one period with high rainfall that effected the clarity of the water. One exceptional day, when the hatches were abundant and the water levels were perfect, allowed one lucky couple to land 40 trout in one day - certainly our record so far.
